What is this?

This was a foray for me into the world of Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs). I have been learning about them on and off for a month or so before creating this. When I had the opportunity to put into practice what I had been reading & watching, I jumped at the opportunity. While I am by far not a master of GANs, I certainly have a better understanding of how to properly implement them and how I can improve the next time around!
